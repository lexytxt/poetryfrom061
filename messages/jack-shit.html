Jack shit

Oh, you’re sorry?
“Blah, blah, blah.”
I read you like a book.

What goes around
comes back around—
it’s sinker, line, and hook.

I bet “I don’t care”
don’t feel too good
with the shoe on the other foot.

No, your “sorries”
mean jack shit
next to what’s in it for you.
